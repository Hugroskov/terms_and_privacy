We may use your information to:

Subscription purchases made through Apple are processed by Apple, not directly by Latinovibes. We may receive subscription status and entitlement information from our subscription management providers in order to grant or restore access to premium content.

We may use third-party service providers to help operate the Services, including providers for authentication, data storage, subscription management, analytics, crash reporting, and customer support.

These providers may process information on our behalf only to the extent necessary to provide their services to us and subject to their own privacy and security obligations.
